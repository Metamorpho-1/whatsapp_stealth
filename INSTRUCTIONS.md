# Stealth Chat (System Update) - Instruction Manual

Welcome to Stealth Chat, the ultimate camouflaged WhatsApp client. This app is designed to look like a native system process or a completely different application so you can securely check messages in any environment without raising suspicion.

## Initial Setup
1. Open the application.
2. The UI will initially show "Security Auth Required" with a QR code.
3. Open WhatsApp on your phone -> Settings -> Linked Devices -> Link a Device.
4. Scan the QR code displayed on your screen.
5. Once scanned, the app will securely cache your session. You won't need to scan again unless you manually log out from your phone.

## Stealth Controls
The app runs completely in the background without a dock icon.
- **Open/Close App:** Press `Option + Space` (Mac) or `Alt + Space` (Windows) anywhere on your computer to instantly spawn or hide the chat window.
- **Hide Quickly:** Press `Esc` while the chat is open to instantly vanish the window.
- **Panic Mask (Boss Key):** If someone walks up behind you while the chat is open, press `Ctrl + Space`. The chat will instantly be replaced by a fake, scrolling "System Diagnostics Compiling" terminal screen. Press `Ctrl + Space` again to return to the chat.

## Disguising the App (Skins)
Click the **Palette Icon** next to the chat input to cycle through camouflage skins:
1. **Glass:** Invisible frosted glass. Adapts to the color of the app behind it (Mac only).
2. **Terminal:** Hacker monospace green. Perfect if you have a code editor open.
3. **Notes:** Yellow notebook paper with serif font. Great for studying.
4. **Ghost:** 100% invisible UI. Only the text floats directly on top of your screen.
5. **VS Code:** Mimics a dark mode code editor.
6. **Slack:** Mimics the purple corporate chat UI.
7. **Excel:** Mimics a spreadsheet with grid lines.
8. **Notion:** Minimalist white background.

## Sending Files and Emojis
- Click the **Paperclip Icon** to securely select and send photos, videos, or documents from your hard drive.
- Click the **Smiley Icon** to insert emojis.
- Click the **Sticker Icon** to view your saved sticker stash. (Right-click or tap "Save" on received stickers to add them to your stash).

## Secret Notifications
When the app is hidden (`Esc`) and you receive a message, a standard OS notification will appear.
To maintain stealth, the notification is disguised as:
- **Title:** System Process
- **Body:** Background sync complete.

This ensures that even if your screen is being shared or someone is looking at your computer, they will just think it's a generic OS update alert.
